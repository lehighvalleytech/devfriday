12 Days of Coworking

Free Coworking at the Hive every day in March!

The Details

In the spirit of Developer Friday, the Hive will provide free coworking all of March. Then get your coworking card punched 12 days, and get a year of coworking for $100.

The Fine Print

$100 offer is good for new members, prepaying for the year. Current members qualify for a $200 prepaid year with 12 punches.
