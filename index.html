#DevFriday

Developers of all types - cubical dwellers, startup ninjas, freelancing mercenaries - cowork together the first Friday of each month.

RSVP On Meetup

So what is it?

Developer Friday provides a chance to meet fellow developers, share ideas, get feedback, and solve problems.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, your work, and - as long as they come willingly - a few co-workers (if you have them).

Where is it?

Thanks to the Hive for providing the space, as well as internet access, snacks, and beverages throughout the day.

But that's too far...

Think this is a good idea, but you're not in the Valley? Find a space to host it, then fork this site (and make it better).
